Who is Barseana?
(pronounced Bar-shawn-a)


Greetings! My name is Barseana Simond. I am a Full Stack Developer. I enjoy developing ideas and solutions. I live by the phrase, "There is more than one way to skin a cat." In my free time, I enjoy vegetable gardening and volunteering at local non-profit organizations. While volunteering, I was able to travel to several small countries. Traveling has taught me to minimize and not take things for granted. Minimizing has also helped me with coding by fully utilizing what I have available.
